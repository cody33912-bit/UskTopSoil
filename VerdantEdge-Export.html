Our Story

A Family Business Built on Local Trust

VerdantEdge started as a father-and-son operation in the Valleys over twenty years ago. Today, we're a tight-knit team of eight — landscapers, horticulturists, and tree surgeons — all from South Wales.

We're fully insured, NPTC qualified for tree work, and we never subcontract. When you hire us, you get us — the same team from first visit to final clean-up. That's why over 90% of our work comes from referrals and repeat customers.
